Research,Intern,Volunteer & Explore!

Volunteering, researching and being an intern with Grassroots Economics is a rewarding experience that can change the lives of people in need. You will be building a bridge between cultures and sharing your hope, enthusiasm, skills and dreams. Through this experience you will be able to fully understand how Economic Commons and Community Asset Vouchers work and be able to take back and spread this knowledge with you.
